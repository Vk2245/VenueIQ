🚀 **Core Features Built for PromptWars:**
1. Crowd Management: Gemini 2.5 Flash powers real-time density assessment and identifies bottleneck zones from text/image descriptions.
2. Queue Predictions: Live tracking of waiting queues with AI-generated peak-time forecasting to reduce attendee friction. 
3. Real-time Incident Coordination: Multilingual (EN/HI) AI routing of emergencies prioritizing "Medical" and "Overcrowding" directly to specialized staff via push notifications.
4. NanoBanana Integration: Stunning immersive thermal visualizations powered by Google DeepMind's Imagen 3 inside the dashboard.
